# **Prandium — Simple Menu Digitizer**

*A web app that converts any restaurant menu into a structured digital format. Upload your menu (PDF, image, or text), get a clean JSON file ready for online ordering, delivery apps, or your website. Perfect for restaurants, food trucks, and any food business going digital.*

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**Prandium** is a simple menu digitization tool that helps food businesses go online quickly. Upload any menu format (PDF, image, or text), and get a structured digital version that works with online ordering systems, delivery apps, and websites. No technical skills needed - just upload and download your digitized menu in seconds.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

People struggle with menu digitization because:
- **Paper menus** - Can't use for online ordering or delivery apps
- **Messy formats** - PDFs and images are hard to convert to structured data
- **Expensive services** - Professional digitization costs hundreds of dollars
- **Manual work** - Typing menu items takes hours and leads to errors
- **No simple tools** - Existing solutions require technical expertise

---

## 3. Solution Overview

Prandium provides instant menu digitization:
1. **Upload Menu** - Drag & drop PDF, image, or paste text
2. **Extract Data** - Automatic parsing of items, prices, and categories
3. **Review & Edit** - Check and correct extracted information
4. **Download JSON** - Get structured data ready for any platform

**MVP Features:**
- Menu upload (PDF, image, text)
- Automatic item and price extraction
- Category detection and organization
- Review and edit interface
- JSON export with standard format
- No account required for basic use

---

## 4. User Workflow

1. **Upload** - Drag menu file to browser or paste text
2. **Process** - Automatic extraction of menu items
3. **Review** - Check and edit extracted data
4. **Download** - Get JSON file ready for use

**Example Use Cases:**
- **Restaurants**: Convert paper menus for online ordering
