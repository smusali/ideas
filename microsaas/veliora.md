# **Veliora — Simple Form Automation Tool**

*A web app that helps you fill out repetitive online forms automatically. Just enter the form URL, map your data fields, and get a one-click solution to fill forms instantly. Perfect for small businesses, freelancers, and anyone tired of manual data entry.*

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**Veliora** is a simple form automation tool that saves you hours of repetitive data entry. Enter any online form URL, map your data fields, and generate a one-click solution to fill forms automatically. No coding required - just point, click, and automate. Perfect for job applications, contact forms, surveys, and any repetitive form filling task.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

People struggle with repetitive form filling because:
- **Time consuming** - Hours spent filling out the same forms
- **Error prone** - Typos and mistakes in manual entry
- **Boring work** - Repetitive tasks that could be automated
- **No simple tools** - Existing automation requires coding skills
- **Lost productivity** - Time that could be spent on more valuable work

---

## 3. Solution Overview

Veliora provides instant form automation:
1. **Enter URL** - Paste any online form URL
2. **Map Fields** - Match your data to form fields
3. **Generate Script** - Get a one-click automation
4. **Use Anywhere** - Run the automation on any form

**MVP Features:**
- Form URL input and validation
- Field mapping interface
- One-click automation generation
- Save and reuse automations
- Export as bookmarklet or script

---

## 4. User Workflow

1. **Input Form** - Enter the URL of the form you want to automate
2. **Map Data** - Match your data fields to form fields
3. **Generate** - Create a one-click automation
4. **Use** - Click to fill the form automatically

**Example Use Cases:**
- **Job Seekers**: Automate job application forms
- **Freelancers**: Fill out client contact forms
- **Small Business**: Automate lead generation forms
- **Students**: Fill out scholarship applications
- **Anyone**: Automate any repetitive form filling

## 10. Risk Mitigation

**Technical Risks:**
- Form changes - Manual remapping and update notifications
- Browser compatibility - Test across major browsers
- Security concerns - Clear data policies and secure handling

**Business Risks:**
- Low adoption - Focus on specific use cases
- Competition - Emphasize simplicity and accessibility
- Feature creep - Stay focused on form automation

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Start with MVP, iterate based on feedback
- Focus on job seekers and freelancers initially
- Build in public to generate interest
- Offer excellent free tier to drive adoption
