# WeatherWise

> *"Contextual weather insights for your events, photos, and posts."

---

## Overview
WeatherWise is a web app that provides instant weather summaries and contextual annotations for any event, photo, or social post. Designed for solo founders to build in a week, it helps users add relevant weather context to their memories or plans.

---

## Problem
- People want to remember or share the weather for special moments (events, photos, posts).
- Event planners need quick weather summaries for upcoming dates.
- No easy way to annotate content with accurate weather data.

---

## Solution
A web app that:
- Lets users enter a date, time, and location (or upload a photo with metadata).
- Instantly generates a weather summary or annotation for that context.
- Provides shareable weather badges or snippets for social media, blogs, or event pages.

---

## MVP Features (7-day build)
- Date/time/location input or photo upload (EXIF extraction)
- Weather data lookup and summary generation
- Annotation/badge generator for sharing
- Simple history of generated summaries

---

## Monetization
