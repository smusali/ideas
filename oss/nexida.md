# **Nexida — An LLM-Powered Open-Source Platform for Transforming Campus Life**

---

## **1. Problem Statement**

University communities — students, faculty, and staff alike — struggle with:

| Challenge                            | Pain Points                                                                                                                                                    |
| ------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Information Overload**             | • Scattered data across registrar, financial aid, housing, LMS, etc.<br>• Inconsistent messaging channels<br>• No personalized alerts or updates               |
| **Administrative Complexities**      | • Labyrinthine forms and workflows<br>• Manual scheduling and resource booking<br>• Siloed document repositories<br>• Slow departmental response times         |
| **Academic & Personal Support Gaps** | • Limited 24-hour academic help<br>• Difficulty locating research materials<br>• Sparse career & wellness guidance<br>• Under-resourced mental-health services |

---

## **2. Solution Overview — Nexida**

**Nexida** is a campus-wide, AI-first assistant that fuses every university system into a single conversational and visual hub. Driven by large-language-model intelligence, it delivers curated knowledge, streamlines bureaucracy, amplifies learning, and nurtures well-being for all campus roles.
