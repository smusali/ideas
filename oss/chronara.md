# **Chronara**

*A Community-Driven LLM-Powered Time Intelligence Platform for Educators*

---

## 1 🚀 Executive Summary

Chronara is an open-source initiative that reimagines clock-in/out and timesheet workflows for educators through large-language-model (LLM) intelligence. It transforms raw “punch” data into rich insights, automates anomaly resolution, and surfaces actionable schedule analytics—all while remaining transparent, privacy-preserving, and extensible for any learning environment.

---

## 2 🎯 Problem Statement

Educators juggle dynamic schedules: arrivals, departures, lunch breaks, extracurricular duties, and split-site assignments. Existing timesheet tools record discrete timestamps but offer little contextual understanding. Common pain points include:

| Pain Point                                                   | Impact                                                                       |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Mis-clocking (forgotten punch, double punch, wrong location) | Payroll disputes, manual corrections                                         |
| Fragmented break tracking                                    | Compliance risk with labor regulations                                       |
| Limited multi-user support                                   | Administrative overhead at larger centers                                    |
| Sparse analytics                                             | Little insight into staffing patterns, overtime drivers, or ratio compliance |

---

## 3 💡 Chronara’s Vision

Chronara aims to become the **“time intelligence layer”** atop any attendance or HR system for the education sector, enabling:

1. **Context-Aware Logging** – Enrich raw punches with shift intent, activity tags, and location context inferred from natural-language prompts or sensor telemetry.
2. **LLM-Assisted Corrections** – Allow teachers to *explain* discrepancies (“I forgot to clock back in after fire drill”) and auto-generate proposed edits for admin review.
3. **Proactive Validations** – Detect illogical sequences (overlapping punches, negative durations, policy breaches) and suggest fixes before payroll lock.
4. **Adaptive Insights** – Generate plain-language summaries of staffing coverage, break compliance, and utilization trends for directors.
5. **Community-Extendible Rulesets** – Let operators publish or import rule packs (e.g., regional labor laws, union agreements) that Chronara’s reasoning engine can apply.

## 5 🧠 LLM-Powered Capabilities in Depth

1. **Natural-Language Data Entry**

   * *Example*: “Clock me out for lunch at 12:05, back in at 12:40.” → auto-creates split entries.

2. **Anomaly Explanation & Autofix**

   * Detects 25-hour shift → generates suggestion: “Likely forgot to clock out yesterday at 17:00. Insert missing clock-out?”

3. **Policy Reasoning**

   * Applies rule pack: “Minimum 30-minute unpaid break after 5 hours.” Flags violations; drafts schedule adjustments.

4. **Narrative Analytics**

   * Converts metrics into story form: “Monday saw 3 instances of ratio risk in Toddler Room; each lasted under 10 min.”

5. **Conversational Querying**

   * Directors ask: “Show teachers with >8 h overtime this month” → receives list plus human-readable context.

---

## 8 🛡️ Privacy & Security

* **Principle of Least Data** – Collect only timestamps, no biometric data unless explicitly enabled.
* **Edge Anonymization** – Strip PII before sending snippets to third-party LLM providers or enable self-hosted inference.
* **Transparent Prompt Logging** – Store prompts/responses for audit, with opt-out controls.
* **Role-Based Access** – Fine-grained permissions for viewing, editing, and approving entries.
